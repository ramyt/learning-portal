🔤 NEW — Phonics: 4 Sounds in Food Words
Listen and repeat — copy the mouth shape!
🔴
Short A Sound — sandwich · salad · jam
Wide open mouth — tongue low and flat
🟢
Long EE Sound — tea · meat · pea
Spread lips wide like a big smile — hold it longer
🔵
Short O Sound — coffee · hot · pot
Round lips like a small circle — short and quick

☕ Ordering in a Café
Can I have a coffee, please?
我能要一杯咖啡吗？
I would like the soup, please.
我想要那个汤，谢谢。
What does ___ taste like?
___尝起来是什么味道？
Is it spicy / sweet / hot?
它辣/甜/热吗？
Can I have the bill, please?
能给我账单吗？
Thank you, that's all.
谢谢，就这些。
