🤝 Café Role-Play with Your Partner
English only · take turns as waiter and customer
20 min
📋 How to do the role-play tonight
  • Round 1: Your partner is the waiter. You order a full meal — 3 food items + 1 drink
  • Use: "Can I have a ___, please?" and "What does ___ taste like?"
  • Partner answers: "It is ___ and ___." (use taste adjectives)
  • Round 2: Swap — you are the waiter. Partner orders in English.
  • Waiter challenge: say "Sorry, we don't have that — what else would you like?"
⚠️ English only for the whole role-play. If you forget a word — describe it! "It is the hot drink... it is black..." (= coffee)
