🎤 Hypothetical Chain — Voice Note
3 minutes · 4-step conditional chain · no script

Topic: "If I could completely change my career, what would I do — and what would happen next?"
  • 🔗Build a chain: "If I could change career, I would become a ___." → "If I became a ___, I would..." → keep going!
  • 📐Use second conditional throughout — no first conditional slip-ups
  • 💼Use at least 2 of the 4 new Week 2 idioms naturally in the chain
  • 🔀Include at least one polite request form: "I was wondering if I could..."
  • 🚫No notes — think and speak

🤝 Hypothetical Question for Partner
English only · you lead
15 min
📋 Tonight's paired practice
  • Ask your partner: "What would you do if you had one million dollars?"
  • She will find this hard — help with simpler English, not Mandarin
  • Model the second conditional for her: "If I had a million dollars, I would..."
  • Ask 2 more hypothetical questions — keep it lighthearted and fun
  • Then swap — she asks you. Give full second conditional answers as a model.
⚠️ This is excellent modelling practice for you — she learns second conditional by hearing you use it correctly 5+ times. She doesn't need to produce it perfectly yet.
