🤝 Ask Your Partner About His Plans
English only · 15 minutes
15 min
📋 Tonight's paired practice
  • Ask your partner: "What do you want to do this year?"
  • Listen carefully. Ask a follow-up question: "Why? Tell me more."
  • Ask: "What are you going to do this weekend?"
  • Ask: "What do you hope to do someday?"
  • Now swap — he asks you the same questions. Use full sentences with time expressions.
⚠️ English only for 15 minutes. If you forget a word — use a different word or describe it. No Mandarin!
