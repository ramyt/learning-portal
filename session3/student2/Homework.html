🤝 Opinion Exchange with Your Partner
English only · you pick the topic
15 min
📋 Tonight's paired practice
  • Choose a topic you both have opinions on (food, travel, technology, work)
  • You state your opinion using: "I think... because... For example..."
  • She must respond in English: "I agree/disagree because..."
  • Help her build longer responses — ask: "Why? Can you say more?"
  • Take turns. Minimum 3 exchanges each. English only.
⚠️ Your job: make the conversation stay in English. If she uses Mandarin — gently ask "Can you say that in English?" No translating.
