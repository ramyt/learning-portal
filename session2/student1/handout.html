📅 Time Expressions
🌅
in the morning
"I eat breakfast in the morning."
☀️
in the afternoon
"I have lunch in the afternoon."
🌆
in the evening
"I come home in the evening."
🌙
at night
"I go to sleep at night."
📅
every day
"I take a shower every day."
🕐
at [time] o'clock
"I wake up at 7 o'clock."
💡 Remember
  • Use in with: morning / afternoon / evening
  • Use at with: specific times and "at night"
  • Use every with: day / morning / evening
