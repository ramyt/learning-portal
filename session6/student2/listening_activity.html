🎧 Authentic Listening Notes

Note your answers here as you listen. No pressure — gist first!

🎤 Clip 1
🎯 LISTEN 1 — GIST
1
What is the story mainly about?
2
What problem did Dave Carroll have?
3
How did the situation end (in general)?
🔍 LISTEN 2 — DETAIL
3
What exactly happened to Dave’s guitar, and how much did it cost to fix?
4
What did Dave do after United Airlines refused to help him?
💭 INFERENCE + COMPARISON
5
Why does the speaker say United Airlines were “the bigger losers”?
6
Do you think social media is powerful? Why?
7
What would you do in Dave’s situation? (for production phase)
📰 Clip 2
🎯 LISTEN 1 — GIST (big picture only!)
1
What is the main idea of the talk? (topic in 3–5 words)
2
What does the speaker suggest people should do for 30 days?
3
How does the speaker feel about these 30-day challenges overall?
🔍 LISTEN 2 — DETAIL (hunt for specifics)
4
What are two things the speaker learned from doing 30-day challenges?
5
Why does the speaker say his book is “awful”?
6
What does the speaker mean by “small sustainable changes are more likely to stick”?
💭 INFERENCE (what is NOT said directly)
7
Is he saying big challenges are bad, or just harder to continue?
8
How can you tell? What words or tone gave it away?
