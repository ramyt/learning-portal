🤝 Tell Your Partner a Story
English only · 15 minutes
15 min
📋 Tonight's paired practice
  • Tell your partner a story about last week: "First, I... Then, I... After that, I..."
  • He listens carefully and asks: "Then what happened? How did you feel?"
  • Then swap — he tells you a story about his past week
  • You listen and ask follow-up questions: "Did you... ? Where did you...?"
  • Only English for the whole 15 minutes!
⚠️ Your goal tonight: use past tense the whole time. If you accidentally say present tense, correct yourself: 'Sorry — I WENT, not I go!'
