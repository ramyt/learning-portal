📅 Regular Past Tense — Spelling Rules
Most verbs: add -ed
walk → walked
watch → watched
cook → cooked
talk → talked
Verb ends in -e: add -d only
like → liked
hope → hoped
arrive → arrived
Verb ends in consonant + y: y → i + ed
study → studied
try → tried
worry → worried

📐 Three Past Structures
Type
Formula
Example
✅ Positive
subject + past verb
"I went to the market."
❌ Negative
subject + didn't + BASE
"I didn't go." (not went!)
❓ Question
Did + subject + BASE?
"Did you go yesterday?"
⚠️ Common Mistake
  • ❌ "I didn't went" → ✅ "I didn't go" — always BASE after didn't
  • ❌ "Did she went?" → ✅ "Did she go?" — always BASE after Did

📖 Story Connectors
First,
To begin the story
"First, I woke up at 7am."
Then,
What happened next
"Then, I made coffee."
After that,
The next event
"After that, I took the bus."
Later,
Some time passed
"Later, I met my friend."
Finally,
The last event
"Finally, I came home and slept."
Suddenly,
A surprise
"Suddenly, it started to rain!"
