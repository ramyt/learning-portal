🤝 Speak English with Your Partner
Together at home — English only!
15 min
📋 What to do together
  • Find a quiet time at home — after dinner is good
  • Your partner asks you these questions in English:
    "What is your name?" · "Where are you from?" · "How old are you?" · "How are you?"
  • You answer each question in English. Use your introduction script if you need help.
  • Then you ask your partner the same questions.
  • Try to have a small conversation for at least 15 minutes — only English!
⚠️ Rule: No Mandarin during this 15 minutes. If you don't know a word — try to explain it in English or use gestures!
