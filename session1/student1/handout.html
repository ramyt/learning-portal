👋 My Introduction Script
📢 Say this out loud — practise until you remember it!
1
Hello! My name is ________.
2
I am from China.
3
I am ___ years old.
4
I live in ________.
5
Nice to meet you!
💡 Remember
  • Say I am — not "I is" or "I are"
  • Your name is correct in any language — just say it clearly
  • Speak slowly — that is okay!
📐 Grammar — Verb "to be"
am · is · are
I

"I am from China."
He / She / It
is
"She is a student."
We / You / They

"We are learning English."
❓ 5 Question Words
WHO
FOR PEOPLE
"Who is she?"
Ask about a person
WHAT
FOR THINGS
"What is your name?"
Ask about a thing or information
WHERE
FOR PLACES
"Where are you from?"
Ask about a place or location
WHEN
FOR TIME
"When is your birthday?"
Ask about time or date
HOW
FOR MANNER / FEELINGS
"How are you?"
Ask about feelings or the way something is
💬 Useful Phrases Today
Nice to meet you!
Say this when you meet someone new
I am fine, thank you.
Answer to "How are you?"
Can you repeat that please?
Say this if you did not understand
I don't understand.
Say this if something is confusing
How do you say ___ in English?
Ask your teacher or partner for a word
